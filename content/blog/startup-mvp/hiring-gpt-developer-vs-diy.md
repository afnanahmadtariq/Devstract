# Hiring a GPT Developer vs. DIY: What You Get for Your Money

"I can just build this myself in 10 minutes."

We hear this often. And it's true! You _can_ build a GPT in 10 minutes. But there is a massive difference between a GPT that _works_ and a GPT that _scales_.

## The DIY Ceiling

When you build a GPT via the ChatGPT interface ("Create a GPT"), you are limited to:

1.  **Static Files**: You upload a PDF. If you change your prices, you must manually re-upload.
2.  **Basic Prompting**: "You are a helpful assistant." This breaks easily under adversarial testing (users trying to trick the bot).
3.  **No Middleware**: You connect directly to APIs with no error handling or security layer.

## The Professional Grade Difference

When you hire an agency like Devstract, you aren't paying for "Prompting." You are paying for **Architecture**.

### 1. Dynamic Knowledge (RAG)

We don't upload files. We connect your GPT to a **Vector Database** that syncs with your website/Notion/Google Drive every hour. Your bot is never out of date.

### 2. Guardrails & Security

We implement "constitutional AI" layers that prevent the bot from conducting competitor analysis for users or speaking about controversial topics.

### 3. Analytics

A DIY GPT gives you a "Chat Count." Our custom implementations give you:

- Average Sentiment Score
- Most cited sources
- Conversion tracking (Did the user click the link?)

## ROI Calculation

If your GPT handles 1,000 customer queries a month:

- **DIY Failure Rate**: 15% (Bad answers, hallucination) -> 150 angry customers.
- **Pro Failure Rate**: <2% -> 20 minor issues.
